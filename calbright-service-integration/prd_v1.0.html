1. Background & Problem

Current State

Calbright College's Student Portal currently integrates four partner services: Career Connect (career services), Timely Care (wellness services), My Accommodate (accessibility services), and Canvas (LMS). Students can access these services directly from their portal. However, the Staff Portal has zero visibility into these partner services. When counselors, advisors, and enrollment staff interact with students through the Staff Portal, they cannot see whether a student is using partner services, what their status is, or take action to connect students with appropriate support.

Problems

  • Staff must leave the portal and log into each partner system separately to check student service status or create referrals
  • Counselors preparing for 1:1 sessions have no way to see if a student is actively using Career Connect, Timely Care, or has accommodation plans
  • The Intake Assessment surfaces risk levels (e.g., Personal Wellbeing score 75, Risk: High) but provides no direct referral path to partner services
  • Coordinating multi-service interventions for at-risk students (low attendance, poor progress) requires manual emails and phone calls across departments
  • Enrollment actions (Change Program, Drop/Withdraw) can inadvertently disrupt a student's active accommodation plan because staff have no visibility into My Accommodate

Why Now

  • Student Portal partner integrations are already live — the API connections exist
  • Staff consistently report friction when coordinating across services
  • At-risk student intervention is a strategic priority for the college
  • The Staff Portal student detail page already contains the data framework (Intake Assessment, persistence tracking, attendance) to support intelligent partner service recommendations

5. Detailed Design

5.1 Partner Services Card

Location

Right column of student detail page, positioned above the existing Actions card.

Display

Four service rows showing:

  • Service name and icon
  • Connection status badge (Active / No Activity / Active Plan / Enrolled)
  • Last activity date or description
  • "View" link to open detail drawer

Interaction

Clicking "View" opens a right-side drawer panel (~400px) showing:

  • Service header with status
  • Activity history (last 5 items with dates)
  • For services with "No Activity": warning message + "Send Recommendation" button

Sequence

sequenceDiagram participant Staff participant Portal participant PartnerAPI Staff->>Portal: Opens student detail Portal->>PartnerAPI: GET /status/{studentId} PartnerAPI-->>Portal: Service statuses Portal-->>Staff: Render Partner Services card Staff->>Portal: Clicks "View" on Timely Care Portal->>PartnerAPI: GET /activity/{studentId}/timelycare PartnerAPI-->>Portal: Activity history Portal-->>Staff: Opens detail drawer

5.2 Intake Assessment Referral

Location

New "Actions" column added to the Intake Assessment table.

Trigger

"Refer" button appears on rows where Risk Level = High.

Referral Modal Fields

  • Pre-filled: Category name, Risk Level, Score
  • Service selection dropdown: Career Connect, Timely Care, My Accommodate
  • Notes textarea (required)
  • Priority: Urgent / Normal (radio)

On Submit

Referral sent to partner service API, logged in activity feed, confirmation shown.

5.3 Coordinated Intervention Plan

Trigger

"Create Intervention Plan" button in Risk Indicators card (appears when 2+ risk indicators are in critical range).

Modal Content

  • Student risk summary (attendance, course progress, persistence status)
  • Service checklist with pre-suggested actions:
    • Timely Care — Wellness check-in
    • Career Connect — Career motivation session
    • Counselor — Schedule 1:1 appointment
    • My Accommodate — Review accommodation needs
  • Overall notes textarea

On Submit

Creates plan record, sends referrals to all checked services, single activity feed entry.

5.4 Accommodation Alert

Trigger

Staff clicks an enrollment action (Change Program, Drop/Withdraw, Extend Timeline) for a student with an active My Accommodate plan.

Alert Modal

  • Warning icon
  • Title: "Active Accommodation Plan Detected"
  • Accommodation details (from My Accommodate API)
  • Three options:
    • Proceed & Notify Accessibility Office
    • Proceed Without Notification
    • Cancel

5.5 Workshop → Career Recommendation

Trigger

Workshop status changes to "Completed."

Display

Info banner in Workshops card with recommendation text and "Approve & Send to Career Connect" button.

On Approval

Referral created, banner replaced with confirmation.

7. Edge Cases & Error Handling

Scenario Handling
Partner service API is unavailableShow "Service temporarily unavailable" in Partner Services card; disable referral to that service
Student has no Intake AssessmentHide Referral buttons; Partner Services card still shows status
Referral submission failsShow error message with retry option; do not log in activity feed until confirmed
Student has no active accommodation planEnrollment actions proceed without alert (normal behavior)
Staff tries to refer to a service student is already active inShow info: "Student is already active in [service]. Proceed anyway?" with confirm/cancel
Concurrent referrals from multiple staffLast-write-wins with notification to other staff members
Partner service returns partial dataDisplay available fields; show "Some data unavailable" for missing fields
